Site Name

TrailTime – A name that reflects outdoor exploration, hiking, and biking trails across various landscapes. The name is simple, memorable, and relevant to adventure-seeking users.

Optional domain availability: trailtimeadventures.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of TrailTime is to serve as an online guide for outdoor enthusiasts. The site will provide curated trail recommendations, gear reviews, user-submitted photos, and a forum to connect with fellow hikers and bikers. It also includes a membership option for exclusive trail maps and community meetups.

Scenarios

  • What are the best beginner-friendly hiking trails within 50 miles of my city?
  • Where can I read real reviews of biking gear and accessories before purchasing?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Forest Green (#2E8B57) – Used for headings, buttons, and navigation to represent nature and calmness.

Secondary Color: Light Sand (#F5F5DC) – Used as the background for the page to create a clean, natural aesthetic.

Typography

Headings: 'Poppins', sans-serif – Clean and modern for emphasis and structure.

Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – Easy to read for paragraphs and long text sections.

Special Sections (quotes/testimonials): 'Merriweather', serif – Adds elegance and variety.
